                              Transactions

                              June 24, 1998: Traded by the Atlanta Hawks (as a future 2000 2nd round draft pick) with Cory Carr and a 1999 2nd round draft pick (Lari Ketner was later selected) to the Chicago Bulls for Shammond Williams.

                              June 28, 2000: Drafted by the Chicago Bulls in the 2nd round (34th pick) of the 2000 NBA Draft.

                              March 20, 2001: Waived by the Chicago Bulls.

                              August 2, 2001: Signed as a free agent with the Dallas Mavericks.

                              October 29, 2001: Waived by the Dallas Mavericks.
